Discover Workgroups

Workgroups are the lifeblood of the Jupiter DAO. From generating hype in the Jupiter Discord Server, to creating content, the Workgroups do it all! Meet the cool cats behind Jupiters DAO success below.

Core Workgroup

The Core Workgroup cats manage the core aspects of the Jupiter DAO. From curating the token strict list to moderating and managing the Jupiter discord, the Core Workgroup is on the job.

Communication Workgroup

The cats behind the Communication Workgroup handle all relevant DAO communications. From forming partnerships to leading calls to inform the DAO, these cats do it! Be careful, they may just talk your ear off!

WEN Working Group

The only directive these cats have is to push the vibes to the max. Learn more about their team here.
