FAQs

What is the DAO?

Decentralized Autonomous Organizations (DAOs) represent a system of governance that is decentralized and autonomous. They enable users to collectively reach a consensus on project management, voting, and other important decisions. The Jupiter DAO oversees decisions regarding the LFG launchpad — a platform for launching new project tokens — and the governance of Jupiter's native token, $JUP.

How can I participate in DAO voting?

DAO voting is a crucial component of the Jupiter Project. To get involved, you need to purchase and then stake $JUP. The minimum stake for obtaining voting power is 1 $JUP, which is equivalent to 1 vote. Be advised that staking implies a commitment to lock your $JUP; should you decide to withdraw, there is a 30-day unlocking period. During this period, your voting power diminishes linearly. For instance, if you have 100 $JUP staked and initiate a withdrawal, you lose 3.33 votes per day until the withdrawal is complete.

How do I earn rewards from staking?

Jupiter's staking program, known as "ASR" (Active Staking Rewards), incentivizes users for active participation. To earn rewards, you must vote on proposals, which are announced on the Jupiter Discord #Announcements Channel. By casting your vote on each proposal, you'll accrue rewards. Remember, Voting = Money! Rewards are tallied over a quarterly period and distributed at its conclusion. The inaugural reward period spans from March to June, with a total of 50 million $JUP allocated for distribution among active ASR participants.
