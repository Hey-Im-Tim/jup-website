Sharky AMA Summary

Overview

In the Sharky AMA session, the team outlined their vision to revolutionize the Web3 space by focusing on peer-to-peer financial transactions and simplifying access to capital and lending. They addressed challenges such as regulatory hurdles and resistance from traditional financial institutions, and emphasized the importance of community building and active participation. Sharky plans to expand its offerings beyond NFTs to include real-world assets, aiming to become a liquidity layer for such transactions. The team also discussed upcoming initiatives like an ambassador program and a secret DAO to further engage the community. Overall, Sharky is dedicated to building a strong, active community and navigating the evolving challenges of the crypto landscape.

Vision and Goals

  • Aiming for Revolution: Sharky seeks to revolutionize the Web3 space by embracing the peer-to-peer economy and simplifying financial transactions between individuals.
  • Platform Focus: Currently operating on Solana, the platform primarily focuses on NFTs but plans to expand its offerings.
  • Measure of Success: Success for Sharky would mean making financial transactions as simple as using their platform, allowing easy access to capital and lending.

Obstacles and Token Launch

  • Challenges Ahead: The biggest challenges include the growth of the crypto space, regulatory hurdles, and resistance from existing financial institutions.
  • Token Launch Strategy: Many projects focus too much on price action and incentives rather than building a strong community culture when launching a token.

Community and Ecosystem

  • Building a Strong Community: Sharky emphasizes starting with a core group, expanding strategically, and rewarding active participation.
  • Engagement Plans: The platform has plans for an ambassador program and a secret DAO to further engage and reward its community.

Real-World Assets and Future Plans

  • Aiming for Liquidity: Sharky aims to be a liquidity layer for real-world assets, facilitating loans and mortgages without directly tokenizing or escrowing the assets.
  • Addressing Challenges: The platform seeks to address the challenges of transferring physical assets in a trustless manner and creating reliable systems for tokenizing and redeeming real-world assets.

Overall, Sharky is focused on building a strong, active community, simplifying access to financial services, and expanding its offerings to include real-world assets, all while navigating the challenges of the evolving crypto landscape.
